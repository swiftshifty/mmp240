The City University of New York won two awards in the 63rd New York Emmy Awards, as CUNY TV and the New York City College of Technology each earned honors in Saturday's ceremony. The award for CUNY TV brings to 21 the total number of Emmys the station has been awarded by the National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences New York Chapter.

October 28,2019

The City University of New York has significantly expanded its digital skills curriculum as part of a workplace enviornment that is increasingly reliant on advanced digital technologies across most sectors of the economy. Another aim of this initiative is to encourage traditionally underrepresented groups to take up tech-focused areas of study.
